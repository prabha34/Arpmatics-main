LOGO DESIGN

Logo design is the process of creating a unique, visually appealing symbol or wordmark that represents a brand, business, or organization. A well-designed logo serves as a powerful branding tool, helping to communicate the identity, values, and mission of an entity in a single visual element.

A Wordmark logo is a typographic logo that relies on custom-designed lettering or typography to represent a brand or company.
